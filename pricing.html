Pricing Questions

What's included in the project fee? +

The project fee covers complete development, testing, deployment, documentation, and training. You'll receive a production-ready automation system with full source code access, comprehensive documentation, and the specified support period.

Are there any recurring costs? +

There are no mandatory recurring fees from us. However, you may have costs from third-party services like OpenAI API, n8n cloud hosting, or other integrated platforms. We'll provide a detailed breakdown of all potential ongoing costs during consultation.

Can I upgrade my plan later? +

Absolutely! Many clients start with Starter and upgrade as they see results. We'll credit your initial investment toward the upgrade cost, making it easy to scale your automation capabilities as your business grows.

What happens after the support period ends? +

Your automation continues working independently. You can opt for extended support packages (starting at $500/month) or reach out for specific updates on a project basis. Many clients operate their automations successfully without ongoing support.

Do you offer payment plans? +

Yes! For Professional and Enterprise plans, we offer flexible payment schedules: 50% upfront, 25% at midpoint milestone, and 25% upon completion. Custom payment arrangements are available for Enterprise clients.

What's the typical ROI timeline? +

Most clients achieve positive ROI within 3-6 months. Our automations typically save 60-95% of manual processing time, which translates to immediate labor cost savings and increased capacity for revenue-generating activities.

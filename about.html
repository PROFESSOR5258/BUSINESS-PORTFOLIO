Our Story

Automate Minds was born from a simple observation: businesses waste countless hours on repetitive tasks that could be automated. As an AI engineer working with companies struggling to scale, I saw the same pain points everywhere—manual lead generation, resume screening, data entry, and client onboarding consuming valuable time and resources.

What started as freelance automation projects quickly evolved into something bigger. After building 10+ production workflows that saved clients thousands of hours and dramatically reduced operational costs, it became clear: there was a massive gap between the potential of AI automation and what businesses were actually implementing.

Automate Minds fills that gap. We don't just build automation—we build intelligent systems that learn, adapt, and scale with your business.

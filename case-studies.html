Lead Generation

Intelligent Lead Generation & Automation System

Industry
B2B SaaS
Timeline
3 Weeks
Team Size
1 Engineer
Status
Production

A rapidly growing B2B SaaS company was struggling with manual lead generation processes that were time-consuming, inconsistent, and limited their ability to scale. Their sales team was spending 60% of their time on lead research and initial outreach, leaving little time for actual selling.

The Challenge

  • Manual lead research consuming 20+ hours weekly per sales rep
  • Inconsistent data quality and incomplete lead information
  • High cost per lead ($50-75) limiting growth
  • Slow response times to new leads (24-48 hours)
  • No systematic approach to lead qualification
  • Difficulty tracking lead sources and attribution

Our Solution

  • Automated Google Places web scraping for targeted businesses
  • AI-powered email extraction using GPT-3.5 and LangChain
  • Intelligent lead scoring and qualification system
  • Automated personalized email outreach campaigns
  • Real-time CRM synchronization via API integrations
  • Comprehensive analytics dashboard with Google Sheets

A growing enterprise was receiving 300+ resumes weekly but their HR team could only thoroughly review about 50 per week. This bottleneck was causing them to miss excellent candidates and slowing down their hiring velocity significantly.

The Challenge

  • 300+ resumes per week overwhelming HR team capacity
  • 3-day average time to first review causing candidate drop-off
  • Inconsistent evaluation criteria across reviewers
  • Manual data entry into ATS systems
  • Difficulty tracking candidate pipeline status
  • Missing qualified candidates due to volume

Our Solution

  • Automated resume extraction from Gmail attachments
  • GPT-4 powered resume parsing and data extraction
  • AI-driven candidate scoring against job requirements
  • Automated Google Drive organization and archival
  • Real-time Google Sheets candidate tracking dashboard
  • Standardized evaluation reports with detailed justifications
